# Knowledge Base — Hockley Mint Limited

## About Hockley Mint
Hockley Mint is a well-established British jewellery manufacturer based in Birmingham's
historic Jewellery Quarter. We specialise in crafting bespoke, high-quality jewellery
for retailers and individual customers across the UK and globally.

We are currently developing an AI-powered digital platform that allows customers to
design fully bespoke jewellery through our retailer network — combining traditional
craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology.

## What We Make
- Engagement rings and wedding bands
- Bespoke necklaces, bracelets, and earrings
- Custom signet rings and personalised pieces
- Wholesale jewellery for retailers
- One-of-a-kind commissions for special occasions

## Materials We Work With
- 9ct, 18ct, and 24ct gold (yellow, white, and rose)
- Sterling silver and platinum
- Diamonds, sapphires, rubies, emeralds, and other precious gemstones
- Lab-grown diamonds (eco-friendly and more affordable option)
- Mixed metals and alternative materials on request

## Bespoke Commission Process
1. Initial consultation — discuss your vision, budget, and timeline
2. Design proposal — our craftsmen sketch and propose options
3. Approval and deposit — you approve the design and pay a 50% deposit
4. Crafting — typically 4–6 weeks for bespoke pieces
5. Final delivery — quality checked and delivered with certificate

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I design something completely unique?**
A: Yes. Every piece we make can be fully customised — metal type, gemstone,
   engraving, size, and finish. Our new AI platform will make this even easier.

**Q: How long does a bespoke piece take?**
A: Standard bespoke commissions take 4–6 weeks. Rush orders may be possible
   depending on complexity — ask us for details.

**Q: Do you offer lab-grown diamonds?**
A: Yes. Lab-grown diamonds are chemically identical to mined diamonds but more
   affordable and environmentally friendly. We offer both options.

**Q: Can I see a preview before committing?**
A: Currently we provide hand-drawn sketches and CAD renders for approval before
   crafting begins. Our new AI platform will provide instant 3D visualisations.

**Q: Do you ship internationally?**
A: Yes. We ship to over 30 countries with full insurance and tracking.

**Q: What is your returns policy?**
A: Bespoke pieces cannot be returned as they are made to order. However, we
   guarantee our craftsmanship and will rectify any manufacturing defects.

**Q: How does the AI recommendation system work?**
A: Our upcoming AI system analyses your preferences, budget, and occasion to
   recommend the perfect design. It learns from thousands of previous commissions
   to suggest styles you'll love, personalised to you.

**Q: What occasions do people typically commission pieces for?**
A: Engagement rings are our most popular commission, followed by wedding bands,
   anniversary gifts, birthday presents, and memorial pieces.

## Qualifying Questions Lexi Should Ask
When speaking to a potential customer, find out:
- What is the occasion? (engagement, anniversary, birthday, wedding)
- What is their budget range?
- Do they have a metal preference? (gold, silver, platinum)
- Do they have a gemstone preference? (diamond, sapphire, emerald etc.)
- What is their timeline? (when do they need it by)
- Have they bought bespoke jewellery before?

A lead is ready to book a design consultation when they have shared:
- Their occasion or reason for the piece
- A rough budget (any range is fine)
- A timeline or deadline
